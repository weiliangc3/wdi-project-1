how to play

Avoid red anger boxes

Collect gold karma

Gold karma fills the karma bar. Fill the karma bar for a huge point multiplier

Your karma bar decreases over time, so keep going for karma, if you can

The green bars on the right are your health, you only have once chance, so be careful

In Challenge mode, you must reach a certain amount of points to get to the next stage

In Endless mode, try to reach as high a score as possible
